A Traditional English Tea Room & Licensed Restaurant in the centre of Evesham and tucked away down a quiet courtyard.

The restaurant seats about 50 people with additional seating in the courtyard outside and is housed in a 16th century grade two listed building and has been serving both tourists and locals for more than 10 years.

Tea Rooms and Licensed Restaurant is run by two qualified chefs; Carolanne Ellis and Nigel Pagan who between them have decades of experience. Available is a whole range of mouth-watering meals, sensational sponges, tasty teas and prefect pies.
